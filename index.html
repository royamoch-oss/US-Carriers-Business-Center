A new kind of hub for motor carriers and trucking companies

Motor Carrier Hub is being built as a dedicated online community and data hub for motor carriers and trucking companies. We go beyond one-time compliance services like MCS-150, UCR, VMT calculations, and BOC-3 filings to give you one place to manage your information, stay compliant, and connect with industry-focused tools and resources.

What this hub is designed to do

  • Centralize your company profile – Keep your key carrier information in one secure place.
  • Support compliance – Get guidance and reminders for important filings and updates.
  • Build a carrier community – Connect with a network of carriers and trucking companies.
